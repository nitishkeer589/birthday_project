A Letter From My Heart

My Dearest Jhamma, ❤️🌹

Kabhi kabhi main sochta hoon ki meri zindagi tumhare bina kaisi hoti, aur har baar dil bas ek hi jawab deta hai — adhoori. Tumne meri zindagi ko sirf pyaar hi nahi diya, balki usse ek nayi wajah, ek nayi roshni aur ek nayi khushi di hai. ✨🥰🌷

Tumhari muskaan meri sabse badi kamzori bhi hai aur meri sabse badi taaqat bhi. Tumhare saath bitaya hua har pal mere liye kisi khoobsurat khwaab se kam nahi. Chahe hum hans rahe ho, baatein kar rahe ho, ya sirf ek doosre ke paas khamosh baithe ho — har lamha mere liye anmol hai. ❤️🤍🌸

Tum sirf meri wife nahi ho, tum meri best friend, meri humsafar, meri duaon ka sabse khoobsurat jawaab aur meri zindagi ki sabse pyari kahani ho. Tumhare saath mujhe sukoon milta hai, apnapan milta hai, aur woh ehsaas milta hai jise lafzon mein bayan karna mushkil hai. 💍🫶🌺

Main wada karta hoon ki har khushi mein tumhara haath thaamunga, har mushkil mein tumhare saath khada rahunga, aur har din tumhe yeh ehsaas dilata rahunga ki tum meri zindagi ki sabse khaas insaan ho. Chahe waqt badalta rahe, meri mohabbat tumhare liye hamesha wahi rahegi — sacchi, gehri aur hamesha ke liye. 🌹🤝✨

Thank you for loving me, understanding me, aur meri zindagi ko itna khoobsurat banane ke liye. Agar mujhe dobara zindagi jeene ka mauka mile, to main har baar tumhe hi chununga. Tum meri jaan ho, meri duniya ho, aur meri hamesha wali mohabbat ho. I love you more than words could ever express. ❤️👑💖🦋

Forever and Always Yours,
Nitish ❤️💍✨
